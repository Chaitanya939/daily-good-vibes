Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect only your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter. We do not collect any other personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to send you our daily newsletter at 7 AM ET, which includes:

  • A motivational quote of the day
  • 10 trivia questions
  • 5 key AI news updates

3. Data Storage

Your email address is securely stored in our database (Supabase). We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure database storage
  • Resend: For email delivery
  • Anthropic Claude API: For content generation

These services have their own privacy policies and may process your data according to their terms.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your email address with any third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any newsletter email. Upon unsubscribing, your email will be removed from our active mailing list.

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address as long as you remain subscribed. After unsubscribing, your data may be retained for up to 30 days for record-keeping purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.
